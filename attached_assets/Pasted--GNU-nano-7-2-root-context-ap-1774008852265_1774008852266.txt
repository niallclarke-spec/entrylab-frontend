### Update article (partial update)
PUT /api/admin/articles/:id
Send only fields to change. Publishing auto-pings Google/Bing sitemaps.

### Delete article
DELETE /api/admin/articles/:id

## Valid Categories
broker-guides, broker-news, prop-firm-guides, prop-firm-news, trading-tools, top-cfd-brokers, top-3-cfd-brokers, best-verified-propfirms, new-prop-firms

## Agent Workflow
1. ALWAYS create as draft first (status: "draft")
2. NEVER publish without Niall's approval
3. After creating a draft, send Niall a summary on Telegram with the title, category, and excerpt
4. Wait for approval before updating status to "published"

## Field Notes
- slug: auto-generated from title if omitted. Never change after publishing
- excerpt: 140-160 chars, used in cards and meta previews
- content: full HTML body
- seoTitle: max 60 characters
- seoDescription: 140-160 characters
- author: always set to "Max Powell"
- featuredImage: WebP, 1200x630 recommended
- relatedBroker: broker slug e.g. "gatesfx-review"
- relatedPropFirm: prop firm slug e.g. "ftmo"
